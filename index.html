Alt Extractor

Returns a list of drug names from a denial language (DL) snippet.

All alternatives must be:

  1. In the first paragraph.
  2. Listed after at a colon.

Limitations:

  1. Drugs may be at the end of text
  2. Creates a single list; results are inaccurate if DL contains complex or multiple lists. Be familiar with the DL you are using.
